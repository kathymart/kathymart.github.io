Jellyfish

A lifeless jellyfish still has the capability to sting. Jellyfish are technically sea jellies because they don't have gills like fish. They breathe through their skin. Another fun fact about jellyfish is that they never stop growing.

Orca Whales

Orcas are found in all the oceans. They make three types of sounds: clicks, whistles and pulsed calls. Another interesting fact about orcas is that they have the second heaviest brain among marine animals.
